Welcome To the city Jabalpur
Bargi Dam is one of the first completed dams out of the 30 major dams on the Narmada River in Jabalpur, Madhya Pradesh, India. It was constructed between 1974 and 1990 and has since been a vital source of irrigation and hydroelectricity in the region. The area around the dam has become a popular tourist destination, offering picturesque beauty, serene surroundings, and a rich biodiversity of exotic birds and plants. The Madhya Pradesh Tourism Development Corporation has taken significant steps to promote Bargi Dam as a tourist spot, adding facilities such as accommodation, a restaurant, and a cruise boat. The dam is accessible throughout the year, with the best time to visit being between October and March. Visitors can engage in activities such as bird watching, leisure boating, fishing, water sports, and trekking. The nearest airport is the Jabalpur Airport, which is about 40 km away.
